Phishing

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to steal the usernames, passwords, credit card information and other confidential information by disguising as a trustworthy entity.

Example:

If you get an email from Microsoft requesting to change the password by clicking on a provided link. This could be a phishing attempt. Every key detail of the email will resemble as an original Microsoft email but if you observe closely, there will be certain key factors that are wrong.

Things you need to look for:

  1. Sender E-mail Address
  2. Check for the spelling mistakes
  3. Hover over the content
  4. Be careful when you see the word Urgent!!!
  5. Review the signature
  6. Inspect the headers.

Things to avoid:

  1. Do not click on the links
  2. Do not download any content/attachments
  3. Do not give away any personal information
  4. Don't believe everything you see
  5. Think before you perform any action
